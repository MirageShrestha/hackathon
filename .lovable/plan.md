

## TrueNepal Mustang MVP — Enhanced Reels & Profile

### 1. Reels Section — TikTok/Instagram Style
- **Full-screen vertical reel cards** with gradient overlays on the bottom half
- Side action bar (heart, save, share, comment icons) floating on the right
- Creator avatar + handle overlay at bottom-left
- Swipe/scroll vertically between reels (one reel fills the phone frame)
- Smooth fade/slide transitions with framer-motion
- Travel badges (type, place, duration) as frosted-glass pills on the reel
- Search bar overlaid at top with subtle blur backdrop

### 2. Creator Profile Section — Clean & Polished
- Large avatar with cover/banner area (gradient or mountain-themed)
- Creator name, handle, and short bio
- **Trust badges**: Verified Traveler badge, "X trips shared" indicator, star rating
- **Social links row**: Instagram, YouTube, and a "Message" button
- Grid of their reels shown as thumbnail cards below
- Back navigation and Follow button

### 3. Aesthetic Enhancements — Clean & Minimal
- White space-forward layout with subtle shadows and rounded corners
- Muted earth-tone accents (slate, stone, warm gray) for a travel feel
- Elegant typography hierarchy (larger titles, lighter subtitles)
- Smooth framer-motion transitions between tabs
- Cleaner tab navigation with active indicator animation
- Better card spacing, hover states, and visual hierarchy throughout all sections
- Consistent badge and pill styling across the app

### 4. UX Improvements
- Reel detail panel updates contextually as user scrolls reels
- Wishlist toggle shows filled/unfilled heart with animation
- Better empty states with illustrations/icons
- Smoother page transitions
